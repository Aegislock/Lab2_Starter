Agenda

  1. Review the project goal for the Pomodoro Timer application.
  2. Share initial ideas for features and layout.
  3. Discuss team roles and short-term responsibilities.
  4. Identify blockers and questions for the next meeting.

Quick Notes

The group agreed that the first version should focus on a simple timer, a short task list, and clear controls. A more accessible experience was marked as high priority.

Felix3 suggested an easy-to-read interface, and Felix4 emphasized that screen-reader support is essential.

Unfinished Business

Items carried over from the previous discussion are summarized here.

  • Finalize the exact number of timer presets.
  • Choose whether task history should be saved locally.
  • Confirm if notifications should be included in the first release.

New Business

Feature Ideas Raised During the Meeting

  • Allow users to label each work session.
  • Include a short break and long break mode.
  • Add a visible progress summary for completed sessions.

Miscellaneous Comments, Questions, and Concerns

Should the app open directly on the timer, or should it show a brief welcome screen first?
The team wants to keep the interface friendly for first-time users.

Discussion highlight

The team agreed that the first deliverable should be small and dependable, even if that means postponing optional enhancements.
